> [doi:10.1038/s41467-019-08489-3](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-019-08489-3)

> Peres A, Gidoni M, Polak P, Yaari G. **RAbHIT: R Antibody Haplotype Inference Tool.**
> *Bioinformatics* 35(22):4840–4842 (2019).

```r
citation("rabhit")
```
